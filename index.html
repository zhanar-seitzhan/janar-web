janar seitzhan

Janar Seitzhan is a web-designer from Astana, Kazakhstan. Her novel, Cheat Day, is a dark comedy about disordered eating and desire. (You can buy a copy wherever books are sold, or borrow through most American library systems.)

Janar writes a newsletter called WE GONNA DIE SOON, about absurdity, limitations, and fears of change.

Her web design projects: Yoga Teacher Website, Web Design Portfolio, Online Gallery Shop. Languages: Javascript, Python. Real languages: English, Kazakh, Russian, Italian (A2). Shares her home yoga / contortion practice on Instagram.
